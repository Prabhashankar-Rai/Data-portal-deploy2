- Gross Written Loss Ratio = (SUM(Gross Incurred Claim) / SUM(Gross Written Premium)) * 100
- Net Written Loss Ratio = (SUM(Net Incurred Claim) / SUM(Net Written Premium)) * 100
- Gross Earned Loss Ratio = (SUM(Gross Incurred Claim) / SUM(Gross Earned Premium)) * 100
- Net Earned Loss Ratio = (SUM(Net Incurred Claim) / SUM(Net Earned Premium)) * 100
- Gross Paid Loss Ratio = (SUM(Gross Paid Claim) / SUM(Gross Earned Premium)) * 100
- Net Paid Loss Ratio = (SUM(Net Paid Claim) / SUM(Net Earned Premium)) * 100
- Gross Written Premium Growth = ((SUM(Gross Written Premium CY) - SUM(Gross Written Premium PY)) / SUM(Gross Written Premium PY)) * 100
- Gross Written Premium Achievement = (SUM(Gross Written Premium CY) / SUM(Gross Written Premium Budget CY)) * 100
- Retention Ratio = (SUM(Net Written Premium) / SUM(Gross Written Premium)) * 100
- Number of Policies Renewal Ratio = (SUM(Renewed No of Policies) / SUM(Renewable No of Policies)) * 100
- Gross Written Premium Renewal Ratio = (SUM(Renewed Gross Written Premium) / SUM(Renewable Gross Written Premium)) * 100
- Gross Commission Ratio = (SUM(Gross Commission) / SUM(Gross Earned Premium)) * 100
- Net Commission Ratio = (SUM(Net Commission) / SUM(Net Earned Premium)) * 100
- Combined Ratio Prior to Expense = (Net Earned Loss Ratio + Net Commission Ratio)
- Incurred Frequency = SUM(No of Claims) / SUM(No of Policies)
- Paid Frequency = SUM(No of Claims Paid) / SUM(No of Policies)
- Incurred Severity = SUM(Gross Incurred Claims) / SUM(No of Claims)
- Paid Severity = SUM(Gross Paid Claim) / SUM(No of Claims Paid)
- Written Average Premium = SUM(Gross Written Premium) / SUM(No of Policies)
- Earned Average Premium = SUM(Gross Earned Premium) / SUM(No of Policies)
- Incurred Burning Cost = Incurred Frequency * Incurred Severity
- Paid Burning Cost = Paid Frequency * Paid Severity
